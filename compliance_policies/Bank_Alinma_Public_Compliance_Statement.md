# Bank Alinma — Compliance & Anti-Financial Crimes Division (Public Statement)

Source: https://alinma.com/en/About-the-Bank/The-Bank/Complaince-and-Anti-Financial-Crimes-Division
Retrieved: 2026-07-16

This is a **public webpage summary**, not a downloadable policy document. Bank Alinma
does not publish its detailed internal AML/CFT manual or compliance procedures — see
the Missing Policy Report for that. This file captures what the bank *does* state
publicly, for traceability.

## Core commitments (as published)
- Compliance Department mandate: maintain the bank's reputation/credibility, protect
  shareholder and depositor interests. "Does not tolerate any form of non-compliance
  with policies and regulations."
- Code of Conduct and Ethics: prohibits bribery/corruption; restricts employee gifts.
- Anti-Financial Crime programs cover: money laundering prevention, terrorism
  financing, sanctions screening, commercial concealment prevention, weapons
  proliferation controls.
- KYC: requires verified identity documentation, prohibits relationships with shell
  banks, conducts periodic risk assessments before establishing client relationships.
- Transaction monitoring: electronic systems track suspicious activity using
  money-laundering patterns.
- Whistleblower protection: confidential/anonymous reporting channels.
- Regular compliance training for staff, executives, and board.
